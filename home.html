About Us

Welcome to RAGA Inc

RAGA Inc is a revolutionary online marketplace based in Rhodes University, dedicated to connecting Rhodes University students who are buyers and sellers in a seamless, secure environment. Our platform was created by students for students. Here is some reels to inform you more about the origins of RAGA.


Here is Some more Tea...

RAGA started off as a passion project as a small Instagram page called RU.thriftyy, it then transformed into a Web development project for The Data Mavericks's project for their Web Dev class for 2026. the inspiration of the design of this page is simply for us by us, the youth, the young at heart who are driven to get the bag and get their degree at the same time. This project then became a passion project driven by hunger, drive and commitment. In all honesty, the job market is literally cooked, and the only way to get a job in the Computer Science space is creativity, innovativity and a ton of luck, sprinkled by heart stopping amounts of caffeine and a dab of blood, sweat, tears, sacrifices and more. This website is meant to encompass the pinnacle of the Data Maverick's creativity.


We Hope you enjoy using this website, half as much as we the devs did creating it!
