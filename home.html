Frequently Asked Questions

Is RAGA free to use?

Yes, browsing and listing standard items under R500 is entirely free for students.

Who can join the platform?

Currently, you must have a valid Rhodes University email or student number to participate in trading.

How do I pay for items securely?

Payments are safely handled off-platform (Cash, EFT, or direct transfers) when you physically meet the seller on campus.
